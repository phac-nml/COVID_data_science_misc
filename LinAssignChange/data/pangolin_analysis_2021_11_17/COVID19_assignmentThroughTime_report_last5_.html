Examining changes per lineage with counts versus percentage

One way to flag lineages with less steady assignment in question: - A bionimial test was conducted per lineage with the probability of change set to 10%, and subsequently the Bonferonni multiple hypothesis testing correction was followed. - The scatter plot highlights the percent and count changes per lineage, represented as points. These points are colored by its significance in changes. The color legend reflects the level of significance in change from binomial tests: -log10(Bonferroni-corrected p). Top 10 significant lineages or those with > 100 changes in counts were labeled in the figure.
