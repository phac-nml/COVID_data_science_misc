The script in this markdown is used to generate the Sankey visualization of changes in Pangolin lineage assignment at any point in specificied timepoints, and report top changes in counts and proportions of lineages.

Examining changes per lineage with counts versus percentage

One way to flag lineages with less steady assignment in question Figures labelling top 10 lineages that are significant & those may or may not be significant but with greater than 10% in changes. The color legend reflects the level of significance in change from binomial tests: -log10(Bonferroni corrected p).
